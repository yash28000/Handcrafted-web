About Us:

Welcome to Humaari, your go-to place for celebrating and supporting Aari embroidery! We're all about preserving and spreading the love for this beautiful craft, which thrives in Diyodar, Gujarat. Through Aari, over 300 talented women in Diyodar town have found not just a job, but a way to stand on their own feet and support their families.

At Humaari, we cherish the age-old skills of these amazing women artisans. Our mission? To shout from the rooftops about their talent and dedication! The name "Humaari" says it all - it's Hindi for "ours," symbolising how Aari embroidery is a treasured part of our community's heritage and pride and how it has become a beloved craft for women artisans.

Come join us in celebrating Aari, a craft that's not just theirs, but truly "ours" in every sense of the word!

Our Values

Empowerment

At Humaari, we have empowered over 300 women artisans in Diyodar, Gujarat, providing them with sustainable livelihoods and economic independence through their skilled craftsmanship in Aari embroidery.

Community Support

Every purchase made on Humaari directly contributes to the support and upliftment of the Diyodar community. We believe in creating a social impact.

Preserving the craft

Humaari is dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of Aari embroidery, ensuring that this traditional craft continues to thrive for generations to come.

Ethical and Sustainable

All products at Humaari are ethically produced, with fair wages and working conditions for the artisans involved. We are also committed to environmental sustainability and responsible consumption
